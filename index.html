Hey, I'm Etse

After earning my HCI degree from Brunel University, I explored a different career as a FMCG Account Executive and Manager at a marketing agency. In 2019, I returned to tech and chose design because it is concerned with seeking answers to people's problems. I am fortunate to have worked with leaders who trusted me with big responsibilites early in my design career leading to rapid growth. I’ve managed teams, led projects and shaped processes while contributing to impactful products at startups and large organisations, which has reaffirmed my belief in design’s power to solve meaningful problems.

At work, I'm known for big-picture thinking, attention to detail and for producing thorough work and outcomes. I aim to create solutions that bring ease and value to everyday people and businesses. My strengths lie in strategy, planning, and process creation, and I enjoy the discovery, conceptualisation and ideation stages of design best.
